Dr. Krishna J

Dr. Krishna J

Current Position: Medical Superintendent

Dr Krishna J is currently serving as Medical Superintendent in our Hospital. He is “Ayurveda Vachaspathi” i.e. Post graduate in Panchakarma from institution SDM college of Ayurveda, Hassan and is trained for Traditional Ayurveda Panchakarma and its applications in different disorders under several renowned Vaidyas.

He has served in Academics as Professor for over 10 years. In his 19 years journey, he has served under several domains in Ayurveda and Wellness industry. He was ‘Medical Director’ for distinguished organizations like Premier Vedic Village Spa Resorts, Kolkatta; The white Lotus Global Integrative Healthcare Destination, New Delhi; Shatayu Ayurveda Pvt. Ltd, Bengaluru.

He is personal consultant for Former Prime Minister of India, Former Chief Minister of Karnataka, Former Chief Justice of Karnataka and several Bureaucrats. He has been a key note speaker in both National and International Conferences on Healthcare and Ayurveda.

“Ayurveda Sevak Award”, “Young Achiever” awards are a few of his accolades that he received for his contribution towards the healthcare industry. He had been instrumental in getting first NABH Accreditation for Wellness in the country at the Vedic Village in par with the global standards 2013. He was NABH assessor for wellness centres and was appointed as a member of Technical committee, Accreditation Program for Wellness Centres, NABH- 2012.
