Ramaiah Indic Specialty Ayurveda
Best of Ayurveda - The Authentic Way

Ramaiah Indic Specialty Ayurveda (RISA) specializes in restoration of health at a deeper level - physical ability, balance, long-term dreams, lost harmony and everyday joys. Employing the advancements in medicine and technology and infusing natural and traditional medicine, RISA focuses on not just managing existing conditions, but offers a cure to long-term diseases with holistic healthcare and change of lifestyle. RISA continues to further its growth and research in advanced Ayurveda.
