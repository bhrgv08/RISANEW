SHALAKYA TANTRA (Eye & ENT)

Shalakya Tantra is one of the speciality of Ayurveda focusing on the diagnosis, prevention, and treatment of diseases related to the eyes, ears, nose, throat, and oral cavity. The principles and techniques of treatment described for eye & ENT disorders hold relevance in the management of modern-day diseases as well. Emphasis is also given to preventive measures in-order to maintain the health of the sensory organs and prevent the onset of diseases.

Treatment Approach:

Treatments at our Hospital involves combination of therapies tailored to the individual patient's condition. It adopts a combination of herbal medicines, therapeutic procedures, diet and lifestyle modifications to manage these conditions effectively, aiming to restore the balance and promote overall well-being. Therapies include Nasya (Nasal therapy), Tarpana (pooling medicated ghee over eyes), Anjana (collyriums), Ashchyotana (Eye drops), Karna Purana (ear therapy), Gandusha (mouth gargling), Shirodhara (rhythmic movement of oil/kashaya stream overhead) and other panchakarma therapies.
