KAUMARABHRITYA

Kayachikitsa referred to as internal medicine forms the foundation of this ancient holistic healing system. It is a branch of Ayurveda that focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of diseases affecting the internal organs and systems of the body. Rooted in Ayurveda principles, Kayachikitsa focuses on restoring balance and vitality to the body through personalized treatments tailored to your unique needs. It encompasses a wide range of therapeutic approaches, including Herbal medicines and formulations, dietary and lifestyle recommendations, Panchakarma (detoxification therapies), and Rasayana therapy (Rejuvenation therapies).

Key Principles :

  • Holistic approach
  • Personalized treatment
  • Panchakarma Therapies
  • Preventive and palliative care
  • Wellness therapies

Conditions We Treat

Kayachikitsa addresses a diverse array of acute and chronic conditions, including but not limited to Gastrointestinal, Respiratory, Vascular disorders etc.Contact us today to schedule a consultation and embark on your journey to achieve optimal health and improve quality of life.
