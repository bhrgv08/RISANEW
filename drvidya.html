Dr Vidya

Dr Vidya

Current Position: Senior Ayurveda Physician

Dr Vidya AMR serving as Senior Ayurveda Physician completed her graduation from Government Ayurveda Medical College in 2006 and Post-graduation in Rasashastara and Bhaishajya Kalpana in the year 2020.

She has also worked in a Primary Heath Centre, Tumkur district. She has participated in a number of seminars and presented papers and has a couple of publications to her credit.

At Ramaiah, she coordinated a certificate program for Panchakarma therapists recognised by the National Institute of Open Schooling under the aegis of Ministry of Education, Govt of India. Two batches of therapists passed out under the program.

She was In-charge of the Ramaiah Ayurveda clinic located at Badrappa Layout, Hebbal for couple of months. Currently, she is consultant in Kayachikitsa department of our hospital.
